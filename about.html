Art brings us together.

A statewide creative community rooted in Tamil culture and open to new possibilities.

Our organization

A collective voice for Tamil Nadu’s artists

Founded to connect painters, drawing artists, teachers and institutions, our community creates opportunities for learning, recognition and collaboration. We celebrate classical traditions while welcoming contemporary forms and digital practice.

தமிழகத்தின் கலை மரபைப் பாதுகாத்து, அடுத்த தலைமுறை கலைஞர்களை ஊக்குவிப்பதே எங்கள் நோக்கம்.

Vision

To unite artists across Tamil Nadu and create opportunities for growth and recognition.

✦

Mission

Support artists, promote traditional arts, conduct exhibitions and encourage young talent.

♢

Values

Creativity, inclusion, cultural respect, integrity and meaningful community service.

Future

Our objectives

Building creative opportunity

  • Artist welfare and professional recognition
  • Skill development and mentorship
  • Networking across all 38 districts
  • Preservation of Tamil visual culture
  • Exhibitions and commercial opportunities
Our journey

2015 — Community founded

A shared forum begins with artists and teachers in Chennai.

2018 — Statewide expansion

District coordinators establish a growing regional network.

2021 — Annual art festival

The first flagship showcase welcomes artists across Tamil Nadu.

2025 — Digital transformation

The community begins its journey toward a connected member platform.
